5 Things You Must Do in Seoul

Ride a bicycle along Hangang river

You can cycle at the park along a long stretch of river. Being beside the river, the air is fresh and the breeze is very cooling.
When you get tired of cycling, you can watch dance performances of students mimicking the choreographies of Korean Pop groups.

Buy cosmetics in Myeongdong

Home to many famous local cosmetic brands, buying cosmetics when you visit Korea is a must. Lined side by side, cosmetics stores in Myeongdong compete with one another to give discounted prices which you will never get in other parts of Korea.

Below are a few well known cosmetics brands that you can find in Myeongdong:

  1. Innisfree
  2. Etudehouse
  3. Leneige
